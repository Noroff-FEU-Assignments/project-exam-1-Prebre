About us

Wizards of the Web is created by Kyla, Dónal, Diederik, Eleonóra and Munro, five adults from across the world who found unity and fellowship in their love for Dungeons & Dragons.

Some of us are experienced players, while others are fairly new to the game. However, the ethusiasm is strong among all of us, and after having shared Our ideas With each Other, we decided to take these ideas to the world wide web!
